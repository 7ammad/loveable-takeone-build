## üîÑ Business Workflows

### Talent Onboarding Flow
1. **Registration:** Email signup with verification
2. **Profile Creation:** Complete talent profile with photos/videos
3. **Identity Verification:** Nafath verification for adults
4. **Guardian Consent:** Required for minors under 18
5. **Profile Review:** Automated and manual content review
6. **Activation:** Profile goes live for discovery

### Hirer Onboarding Flow
1. **Company Registration:** Business verification and setup
2. **Subscription Selection:** Choose appropriate plan
3. **Payment Processing:** Moyasar payment integration
4. **Profile Setup:** Company profile and preferences
5. **Search Training:** Learn advanced search features
6. **First Casting Call:** Create and publish first call

### Application Processing Flow
1. **Talent Discovery:** Search and filter talent
2. **Application Submission:** Talent applies to casting call
3. **Review Process:** Hirer reviews applications
4. **Communication:** Built-in messaging system
5. **Decision Making:** Accept/reject with feedback
6. **Placement Tracking:** Monitor successful placements
